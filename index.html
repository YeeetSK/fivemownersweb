Our Community

Our community is made up exclusively of server owners and carefully chosen staff. We encourage open conversations and strictly prohibit any form of hate, discrimination, or similar negative behavior. To help members engage, we introduce a "Weekly Theme" for discussion, though everyone is welcome to talk about other topics as well.

Joining Us

Since the community is strictly for server owners, there are two ways to join:

1. Personal Invitation: I may personally reach out to you on your Discord server, offering an application free entry.
2. Application Submission: You can fill out an application form. Your server must meet certain activity requirements to be considered. However, even if these requirements are met, approval is not guaranteed. You can make an application on our discord.
